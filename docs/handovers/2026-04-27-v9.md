Same as v7. Sketch:

1. Insert `kbt_event` row with `event_code`, `event_description`, `event_date`, `event_status='active'`

3. Open `host-app.html`, enter HOST01, enter event code, Launch

5. End of night → Export to Slides; send wrap links per team
